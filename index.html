THE KREEPY BASEMENT

A deranged killer known only as The Psycho has kidnapped your sister and dragged her into the place locals whisper about — the Kreepy Basement. Sealed beneath a rotting house on the edge of town, no soul has set foot inside for over 100 years. The walls remember things. So does whatever lives in them.

The Psycho leaves no witnesses and no trail — only a scrawled note slipped under your door. You don't know how long she has left. You don't know what waits in the dark. But you know one thing: you're running out of time. Go down. Find her. Don't look back.
